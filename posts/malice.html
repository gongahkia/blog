Keigo Higashino is really a master of his craft. To be perfectly honest, having previously read The Devotion of Suspect X, I was worried Malice would pale in comparison. My fears were unfounded. This was a perfect read in every sense of the word, and I cannot wait to get my hands on his other works. Higashino is truly in a league of his own.

Godspeed.
